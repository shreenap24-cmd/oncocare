What We Offer

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Oncocare is designed around how cancer patients actually live — follow-ups every few months, multiple doctors, and test results that need to be understood, not just stored.

Log blood test results after each follow-up and see whether your markers are improving over time with cancer-specific trend charts.

Appointment Manager

Track every doctor visit — upcoming, completed, or cancelled. Get email reminders the day before so you never miss a follow-up.

AI Health Assistant

The Story Behind Oncocare

Why I built this

"Having witnessed the challenges of managing cancer follow-up care, I wanted to build something that makes the journey a little easier for patients and their families."

Keeping track of appointments, medicines, blood test results, and treatment cycles is overwhelming. Most patients do it through paper notes and memory. It shouldn't be this hard.

Oncocare is my way of turning a personal experience into something useful — a simple, organized place where cancer patients can manage their entire recovery journey.

Shreena
Creator of Oncocare  ·  Healthcare Technology Enthusiast  ·  Software Engineering Student

Built with purpose

Every feature was designed around the real challenges faced during cancer care. Not a generic health app — a cancer-aware companion.

How It Works

Simple to use,
from day one.

You don't need to be tech-savvy. Oncocare is designed to be as simple as writing in a diary — just digital.

1
Create your account

Register with your name and email. Takes less than a minute and is completely free.

2
Set up your cancer profile

Select your cancer type and the system automatically knows which biomarkers to track for you.

3
Log after each visit

After each doctor visit or blood test, log your results. Takes just a couple of minutes.

4
Watch your progress

Your dashboard shows trend charts, upcoming appointments, and a clear picture of your recovery.

5
Share with your doctor

Download your health summary as a PDF and bring it to your next appointment. Everything in one clean document.
