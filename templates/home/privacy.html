1 Overview

Oncocare ("we", "our", or "the platform") is a healthcare companion web application built to help cancer patients manage their recovery journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal and health-related information you provide when using Oncocare.

This platform was developed as a college internship project. It is not a commercial medical application and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

2 What We Collect

We collect only the information you directly provide to us:

Account Information

Username, email address, and password when you register.

Health Information

Treatments, medications, appointments, symptoms, and biomarker readings you log.

Uploaded Files

Lab reports you choose to upload as PDF or image files.

Profile Details

Optional details like date of birth, blood group, phone, and address.

We do not collect any information automatically beyond what is required for the application to function. We do not track your location, browsing behaviour, or device information.

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide you with the features of Oncocare:

  • To display your health data on your personal dashboard
  • To generate biomarker trend charts specific to your cancer type
  • To show upcoming appointments and active medications
  • To allow you to log and review your symptoms over time
  • To store and display your uploaded lab reports
  • To keep your account secure with login authentication

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

4 How We Store Your Data

Oncocare stores all data in a local SQLite database on the server where the application is hosted. As a college project, this runs on a development or demonstration server.

Passwords are never stored in plain text. Django's built-in authentication system hashes all passwords before storing them, meaning even the developers cannot see your password.

Uploaded lab report files are stored in the server's media folder and are accessible only when you are logged in to your account.

5 Sharing Your Data

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal or health data with anyone.

Your health information is visible only to you when you are logged in to your account. No other user can see your data. No third-party companies, advertisers, or data brokers have access to your information.

The only exception would be if we were legally required to disclose information by a court order or applicable law, which is extremely unlikely given the nature of this project.

6 Your Rights

You have full control over your data on Oncocare:

  • Access — You can view all your data at any time by logging in
  • Edit — You can update or correct any information you have entered
  • Delete — You can delete individual records (appointments, medications, readings etc.) at any time
  • Account deletion — You can request your entire account and all associated data to be deleted by contacting us

7 Cookies

Oncocare uses a minimal number of cookies strictly necessary for the application to function:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in while you use the site
  • CSRF token — protects your account from cross-site request forgery attacks

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies. No cookie consent banner is needed because we only use essential cookies.

8 Children

Oncocare is intended for adult cancer patients and their caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

9 Changes to This Policy

As Oncocare develops, this Privacy Policy may be updated. Any significant changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continuing to use Oncocare after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

10 Important Note

Oncocare is a college internship project built for educational purposes. It is not a certified medical device or a HIPAA-compliant platform. Please do not use it as your sole method of managing critical medical information.

Always follow the advice of your oncologist and medical team. Oncocare is a companion tool to help you stay organized — not a replacement for professional medical care.

Questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out. This project was built with care and we take your privacy seriously.
